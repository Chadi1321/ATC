Enrolment Details

Explore the enrolment process and start your educational journey with Australian Training Company.

Application Procedure

Follow these steps to apply for our training programs:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Provide proof of your high school diploma or equivalent.
  3. Submit any additional required documents.
  4. Pay the application fee.

Requirements

To enroll in our programs, you must meet the following requirements:

  • High school diploma or equivalent.
  • Minimum age of 18 years.
  • Basic computer skills (for certain courses).

Key Dates

Our enrolment periods typically start in January and September. Be sure to check our website for specific dates and deadlines.
